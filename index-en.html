About LPK Agira Gakkou

PT. Agira Education Center is a Japanese language training institution focused on basic Japanese language skills as the first step toward working in Japan through the Private Internship Program (SO) and Tokutei Ginou (SSW).

Vision: Produce competent and professional human resources in accordance with Japanese standards.

Mission: Provide Japanese language training and work ethic coaching effectively.

Featured Programs

Private Internship Program (SO)

  • Japanese Language Training N5 – N4
  • Open for Beginners
  • Preparation for Japanese Company Job Selection
  • Training Duration: 2 – 3 Months

Tokutei Ginou (SSW)

  • Japanese Language N4 & SSW Training
  • Intensive training from zero and preparation for JFT & SSW
  • Example Fields: Caregiver, Food Processing, Construction, etc
  • Training Duration: 4 - 6 months
